About Bread Crumbs

What are crumbs?

Crumbs are small pieces of life-bread that leave a trail and will help you remember where you came from and where you want to go. Add a crumb to remind yourself to do something... or simply remember a precious moment, movie fact, or an epiphone!

New Crumbs?

Adding a new crumb will also tag your crumb with date, time, and geolocation. Add a new crumb to remind yourself to do something or leave a trail of something you already accomplished

Ultimate Crumbs

Ultimate Crumbs reminds you of the big goals you set for yourself. You will see the top goal appear on the home page

Current Crumbs?

All My Crumbs brings all your active crumbs onto a single page, prioritizing by importance and by due date

Sweep Crumbs?

This is where you can sweep up those crumbs by completing tasks that you set for yourself. Happy sweeping!

Tasty Crumbs?

Tasty Crumbs can be anything "delicious", from a memorable moment or about a book you read to something you want to do that's fun!

Old Crumbs

Here, you can see all the crumbs that are behind you, in case you want to remember your way back to something.
